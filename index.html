Meet Sai Maddali

I am running for President of Student Government Association (SGA), because I believe that I can make a change in the culture and future service through Student Government. As President, I will advocate for increased student leadership and effectively address my constituents’ concerns. I was raised in Georgia and Downtown Atlanta is my home. I love State with a passion, but I know we can make it better.

Priorities

I love Georgia State University and helping others become passionate about our school. I know that State isn’t perfect, but together, we can create immediate and lasting change.

I promise that the 2018-2019 year of SGA will be filled with quick and decisive improvements to your experience--while also establishing a legacy for the future.
